FAQ

Is this a paid service?

Yes. Conduit is a paid, hands-on service built around your background, target roles, and the companies you want to reach. I'm reviewing early access applications first so I can understand who I'm best positioned to help. Then we'll have a call to evaluate if this is a good fit for both of us before moving forward, and I'll share the scope and pricing clearly before you commit to anything.

Do I need to be technical or know AI?

Not at all. You bring the career context; I handle everything on the AI and strategy side. If you can fill out the form above, you're all set.

Is this only for go-to-market roles?

GTM roles are my sweet spot — Sales, Marketing, BD, Partnerships, Growth — but the core methodology (showing your value before you're hired) works for any role.

Can't I just do this with ChatGPT?

Anyone can prompt a chatbot. The difference is knowing what to build, how to frame it for the specific company and role, and how to present it in a way that changes the conversation. I bring the strategy and the playbook. AI is just the engine. Technically, anyone can do what I'm doing. The difference is almost nobody is doing it, and what's important is learning how, which is what I'm here for.

Is using AI during interviews cheating?

Conduit is not a live meeting assistant that feeds you answers to read back to an interviewer. That's cheating. What I do is help you prepare, using AI to research the company, build a go-to-market strategy, and create proof-of-value materials. That's the kind of initiative employers want to see. You're using AI the same way you'd use it on the job, to do better work, faster. Hiring managers don't penalise preparation. They reward it.

What if I'm currently employed?

You're absolutely welcome. I just prioritise candidates who've been searching longest, as my focus is on helping those in need.

How do I know this is real?

That's what the initial call is for, so you can check whether I'm AI or really human, and decide if this is a fit for you. Conduit is built on a simple premise: show, don't tell. The materials I create demonstrate your value so clearly that interviews follow. I let results do the talking.
